Qualifications in Sailia let you track what staff are certified to teach and what customers have achieved. You can require qualifications to book a session, restrict who can instruct, and record certificate numbers as customers progress.

## Set up your qualifications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the qualifications you issue">
    Go to **Settings → Qualifications** and create each qualification you award, train towards, or accept as a prerequisite. For example: `RYA Stage 1 Sailing`, `RYA Powerboat Level 2`, `RYA Dinghy Instructor`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use them on activities">
    When you create or edit a [course](/activities/activities), set:
    - **Required staff qualifications** — who can run the session, with an instructor-to-participant ratio (e.g. 1 RYA Start Windsurf Instructor per 6 participants).
    - **Required customer qualifications** — what a customer needs to hold before they can book.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Qualifications can be assigned to both customers and staff. The same record type is used everywhere — it makes staffing requirements and customer prerequisites consistent.
</Note>

## Adding qualifications to a customer

You can record a qualification on a customer's profile in two places:

- **Register view** on the [bookings dashboard](/activities/managing-bookings) — at the end of a session, tick off who qualified and enter their certificate number.
- **Customer profile** — open the customer, choose the family member, and add a qualification directly.

Once recorded, the qualification appears on the customer's profile with the certificate number and the date it was awarded.

## Recording certificate numbers

For courses that award a certificate (RYA, BCU, NPLQ, and others), you can record the certificate number when you log the qualification. This means:

- You have an auditable record of who received which certificate
- The certificate number is included on customer-facing emails and exports
- You can search and filter for it later

To record a number:

1. Open the **register** for the session on the bookings dashboard.
2. Click the qualification awarded to a participant.
3. Enter the certificate number and save.

You can also add a number after the fact from the customer's profile.

## Qualifications on staff

When a staff member has a qualification on their profile, Sailia can:

- Show only qualified staff when you assign instructors to a session
- Remind you when a session needs an instructor with a specific qualification
- Help you avoid scheduling someone for a session they're not certified to teach

Manage staff qualifications from **Staff → [staff member] → Qualifications**.

## Restricting bookings by qualification

For activities with a required customer qualification, Sailia will:

- Hide the activity from customers who don't hold the qualification, or
- Show the activity but block the booking until the qualification is added to the customer's account

This is useful for progression pathways — for example, requiring **Stage 1 Sailing** before a customer can book **Stage 2**.
