---
title: "Expected payouts"
description: "Preview upcoming payouts and their transactions before they arrive in your bank account"
---

The financial dashboard shows expected payouts alongside your completed payout history. Expected payouts give you a forward-looking view of funds that are on their way to your bank account, so you can plan your cash flow without waiting for Stripe to finalize each payout.

## How expected payouts work

Sailia calculates expected payouts by looking at card payments that have cleared but have not yet been included in a finalized Stripe payout. These payments are grouped by their expected arrival date, and the projected net amount is displayed in your payouts list.

Expected payouts appear in two forms:

| Type | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Existing** | A payout that Stripe has already created and is in transit or pending. The amount comes directly from Stripe. |
| **Projected** | A payout that Sailia estimates based on unassigned card payments grouped by their available-on date. The amount is calculated from your transaction data. |

Both types appear in the payouts table with an **Expected** status badge so you can distinguish them from completed payouts.

<Note>
  Projected payouts are estimates based on current transaction data. The final payout amount may differ once Stripe processes the payout, as additional charges or refunds may be included.
</Note>

## Viewing expected payout details

Click any expected payout row in the payouts table to see a breakdown of the individual transactions that make up that payout. The detail view shows:

- **Projected net** — the estimated total payout amount after fees
- **Expected date** — when the payout is expected to arrive
- **Transaction list** — each charge or refund contributing to the payout, including the gross amount, net amount, and linked basket

You can click a basket ID in the transaction list to open that basket's details directly.

## Expected vs. completed payouts

Expected payouts are sorted by arrival date alongside your completed payouts. As Stripe finalizes each payout, the expected entry is replaced by the actual payout record with its final amount and status.

| Field | Expected payout | Completed payout |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Status** | Expected | Paid, Pending, In transit, or Failed |
| **Amount** | Projected net from transaction data | Final amount from Stripe |
| **Payout ID** | Not assigned | Stripe payout ID |
| **Accounting sync** | Not available | Synced or Not synced |

<Info>
  Expected payouts cannot be exported or selected for product breakdown exports. Only completed payouts with a valid payout ID are included in exports.
</Info>

## Permissions

To view expected payouts, you need the **View financial dashboard** permission. This is the same permission required to view completed payouts and balance information.
