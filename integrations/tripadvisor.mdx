[Tripadvisor Experiences](https://www.tripadvisor.com) makes your activities bookable directly from your Tripadvisor business listing. The Sailia integration syncs availability and pulls bookings into your dashboard.

Tripadvisor Experiences runs on the same connectivity as [Viator](/integrations/viator), but suppliers sometimes manage products separately. If you have a Tripadvisor Experiences account, follow this guide.

## Before you start

You'll need:

- An active Tripadvisor Experiences supplier account
- API connectivity enabled (you may need to open a support ticket with Tripadvisor)
- Your Tripadvisor product IDs

## Connect Tripadvisor

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find your product codes">
    Log in to your Tripadvisor Experiences dashboard and open each product. Copy the **Experience ID / Product Code** for each one you want to connect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Sailia Integrations dashboard">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations** in Sailia and select **Tripadvisor**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map your products">
    For each product, click **Add Product Mapping**, paste the Tripadvisor product code, and save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm with Tripadvisor">
    Some accounts need a support ticket to activate API connectivity. If asked, open a ticket with Tripadvisor requesting activation for "Sailia connectivity".
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What syncs

- **Availability** updates in real time
- **Bookings** appear in your [bookings dashboard](/activities/managing-bookings)
- **Capacity is shared** with all other sales channels
