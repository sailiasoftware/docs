The Google Things to Do integration makes your tours and activities visible across Google Search, Maps, and other Google surfaces — with live pricing, real-time availability, and direct booking links into your Sailia booking page.

## Before you start

You'll need:

- A verified [Google Business Profile](https://www.google.com/business/) for your business
- Your Google Business Profile ID (sometimes called Google Place ID)
- Activities published in Sailia with full descriptions and images

## Connect Google Things to Do

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify your Google Business Profile">
    Claim or verify your business on Google Business Profile if you haven't already. Your business needs to appear on Google Maps and Google Search.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy your Business Profile ID">
    Find and copy your Google Business Profile ID (or Place ID) from your business profile settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Sailia Integrations dashboard">
    In Sailia, go to **Settings → Integrations** and select **Google Things to Do**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your Business Profile ID">
    Enter your Google Business Profile ID and save. This links your Sailia inventory to your Google Business listing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Google to review">
    Once mapping is complete and Google approves your products, your activities start appearing across Google Search and Maps Things to Do modules. This usually takes up to 72 hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What gets surfaced

When customers see your activities on Google, they can:

- See live pricing and availability
- Click through to your Sailia booking page
- Complete the booking through your normal checkout

Bookings appear in your [bookings dashboard](/activities/managing-bookings) as standard direct bookings from your booking page.

## Improving visibility

Activities with complete information rank better. Make sure your activities have:

- Clear, descriptive titles
- High-quality images
- Detailed descriptions with what's included and what to bring
- Accurate pricing and availability
