Sailia is a booking and operations platform for activity providers. It handles everything from online bookings and point-of-sale transactions to staff scheduling, payments, and accounting.

Here is a quick tour of the main areas you will work with.

## Bookings

Customers book activities — courses, hires, and events — through your online booking page or in person at the point of sale (POS). Every booking flows into a shared order system that tracks payment status, attendee details, and any add-ons.

## Point of sale

The built-in POS lets you sell shop products, process walk-in bookings, and apply discount codes on the spot. You can also set [custom prices](/booking-and-sales/custom-pricing) on individual items when the standard price does not apply.

## Staffing

Assign staff to bookings, set pay rates, and let Sailia [generate timesheets automatically](/staff-and-operations/auto-timesheets) from scheduled work. Staff see their upcoming shifts and you get accurate payroll data without manual entry.

## Payments and discounts

Sailia processes payments through Stripe. You can create [discount codes](/booking-and-sales/discount-codes) with fine-grained controls — usage caps, date windows, early-bird and surge pricing, product restrictions, and tiered amounts.

## Integrations

Connect Sailia to external tools to automate your back office:

- **Xero** — [sync payouts](/integrations/accounting-sync) as categorized invoices with full VAT breakdowns.

## API

Build on Sailia with the REST API. Record [metered usage events](/api-reference/meter-tracking), manage baskets, and react to webhooks.
