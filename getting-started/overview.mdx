Sailia is a booking and operations platform for activity providers. It handles everything from online bookings and point-of-sale transactions to staff scheduling, payments, and accounting.

Here is a quick tour of the main areas you will work with.

## Activities and scheduling

[Activities](/booking-and-sales/activities) are the core of Sailia — courses, hires, and events that customers can book. Each activity has its own schedule, pricing, capacity, and staffing. Customers book through your [online booking page](/booking-and-sales/online-bookings) or in person at the [point of sale](/booking-and-sales/point-of-sale). Every booking flows into a shared order system where you can [manage reservations](/booking-and-sales/managing-bookings), track payment status, and update attendee details.

You can also offer [memberships and activity passes](/booking-and-sales/memberships-and-passes) for recurring access and prepaid bundles, sell [gift cards](/booking-and-sales/gift-cards), and manage [waitlists](/booking-and-sales/waitlists) for fully booked sessions.

## Point of sale

The built-in [POS](/booking-and-sales/point-of-sale) lets you sell [shop products](/booking-and-sales/shop-products), process walk-in bookings, and apply discount codes on the spot. You can also set [custom prices](/booking-and-sales/custom-pricing) on individual items when the standard price does not apply.

## Pricing and promotions

Sailia gives you flexible pricing tools. Create [discount codes](/booking-and-sales/discount-codes) with fine-grained controls — usage caps, date windows, early-bird and surge pricing, product restrictions, and tiered amounts. Sell [gift cards](/booking-and-sales/gift-cards) that customers can redeem at checkout, and offer [memberships](/booking-and-sales/memberships-and-passes) with auto-applied discounts.

## Customers

[Manage your customers](/customers/overview) from a central dashboard — view booking history, check membership status, and track waiver completion. Sailia sends [automated notifications](/customers/notifications) for booking confirmations, waitlist updates, and membership renewals. Collect signed [waivers](/booking-and-sales/waivers) and supporting documents before activities, with support for digital signatures, checkboxes, and [file uploads](/booking-and-sales/file-uploads).

## Staffing

[Manage your team](/staff-and-operations/staff-management) by assigning staff to bookings, setting pay rates, and controlling permissions. Let Sailia [generate timesheets automatically](/staff-and-operations/auto-timesheets) from scheduled work. Staff see their upcoming shifts and you get accurate payroll data without manual entry.

## Payments

Sailia processes [payments through Stripe](/payments/stripe) — handling online checkouts, POS card payments, refunds, and payouts.

## Integrations

Connect Sailia to external tools to automate your back office:

- **Xero** — [sync payouts](/integrations/accounting-sync) as categorized invoices with full VAT breakdowns
- **Workflows** — set up [automated emails](/integrations/workflows) triggered by booking events

## API

Build on Sailia with the REST API. Record [metered usage events](/api-reference/meter-tracking), manage baskets, and react to webhooks.
