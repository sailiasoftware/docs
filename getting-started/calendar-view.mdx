The **What's On** calendar is the main view of your schedule. You can switch between three views depending on how much detail you need to see.

## Switching views

Under the mini-calendar on the left of the **What's On** screen, use the toggle to switch between:

| View | Best for |
| --- | --- |
| **Day** | A detailed hour-by-hour view of a single day, including overlapping sessions |
| **Week** | A seven-day overview showing each day's sessions side by side |
| **Month** | A high-level overview of the whole month — the default view |

## Seeing more sessions on a busy day

When a day has more sessions than fit on screen, a slider appears so you can scroll through the full agenda:

- In **day** view, scroll vertically to see sessions earlier or later in the day.
- In **week** view, the slider appears for any day with more than three sessions.
- In **month** view, hover over a date to activate the slider, then drag it up or down to view the full day.

## Choosing the right view

There is no single "correct" view — each one suits a different task:

- Use **day** view when running operations on the day and you need exact times.
- Use **week** view for planning staffing and equipment for the week ahead.
- Use **month** view for an at-a-glance overview of how busy the centre is.

<Tip>
  Switch views freely throughout the day. Sailia remembers your last-used view per device.
</Tip>
