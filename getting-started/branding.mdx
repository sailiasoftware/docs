Your branding appears on your public booking page, email templates, receipts, and your customer's view of their account. Set it up once in **Business settings** and it carries through everywhere.

## What you can configure

In **Settings → Business**, you can update:

| Setting | Where it appears |
| --- | --- |
| **Logo** | Top of your booking page, in confirmation emails, and on PDF receipts |
| **Favicon** | The small icon in browser tabs when customers visit your booking page |
| **Company name** | Email sender name, receipts, and the title of your booking page |
| **Contact email** | The reply-to address on transactional emails |
| **Contact phone** | Shown on receipts and your public booking page |
| **Website URL** | Linked from your logo and footer on the booking page |

## Upload your logo and favicon

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Business settings">
    Go to **Settings → Business**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your logo">
    Use a horizontal image with clear space around the mark. PNG with a transparent background works best so it sits cleanly on light and dark backgrounds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your favicon">
    Use a square image — ideally at least 256×256 pixels. This is the icon shown in browser tabs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Your changes go live on your booking page within a few seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  See [uploading images](/getting-started/uploading-images) for file size, format, and dimension guidance.
</Tip>

## Update your business details

Update your company name, contact email, contact phone, and website URL in the same **Business settings** screen. These details flow through to your booking page, email templates, and receipts automatically.
