If you already have members in another system, you don't need to recreate them by hand. Sailia handles member migrations as a managed process — we import your members, their plans, and their renewal dates, then notify each member about the switch.

<Note>
  Migrations are handled by the Sailia team. Contact [support@sailia.com](mailto:support@sailia.com) to get started — usually all we need is a CSV export or read-only access to your current system.
</Note>

## What gets migrated

Where the data is available, we import:

- Member names and contact details
- Membership types and plans
- Renewal dates and payment history
- Linked benefits and discounts

Once imported, members appear in your [memberships dashboard](/pricing/memberships) ready to book, renew, and use their benefits straight away.

## What members see

After your import, Sailia automatically sends each migrated member a **welcome email** explaining:

- Their membership has been transferred to Sailia
- They'll need to add their card details to renew when their current term ends
- They'll get reminders in the run-up to their renewal date

The aim is a smooth, transparent switch — no surprise charges, no loss of benefits.

## Migration status badges

A migrated member can be in one of these states on their subscription page:

| Badge | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Migrating (Pending payment info)** | Member hasn't added a payment method yet. We'll keep reminding them. |
| **Migrating (Pending renewal)** | Member is set up and will start billing on their normal renewal date. |
| **Will not renew** | Migration was cancelled; the current term ends without renewal. |

Once a member adds a payment method and their first renewal goes through, they become a standard active member.

## Supported source platforms

We can migrate members from almost any system. We've already built imports for the most common platforms, including:

- Bookwhen
- BookNow
- Eola
- SportBooker
- Roller
- Cinolla
- Checkfront

If your current platform isn't listed, we can usually import from a CSV export or via an API connection — get in touch and we'll work out the right approach.

## Getting started

1. Email [support@sailia.com](mailto:support@sailia.com) letting us know you'd like to migrate.
2. Share an export file or set up read-only access to your current system.
3. We confirm the data, run the import, and trigger the welcome emails.

Most migrations take a few days end-to-end.
