Benefits are what make a membership worth paying for. Sailia supports three benefit types — combine them freely to design your offering.

## Session discount benefits

Apply a percentage or fixed discount to specific sessions or activity types. Use this for things like:

- *20% off windsurfing lessons for members*
- *Free Friday paddleboard hire*

You choose what the discount applies to and how much. Sailia applies the benefit automatically at checkout — members never need to enter a code.

## General benefits

Free-text perks that don't change pricing but still matter to members:

- *Access to the members' lounge*
- *Priority email support*
- *Early access to event bookings*

These appear on the public membership page alongside other benefits, helping customers see the full value of joining.

## Ticket access benefits

Control which tickets members can see or book — or create member-only ticket types. Use this for:

- *Open water swim — members only*
- *Yoga drop-in: £8 for members, £12 for non-members*

You can mix public and member-only tickets on the same session, with separate pricing tiers for each.

## Managing benefits

Open a membership and use the **Benefits** section to add, edit, or remove benefits. Changes apply to every member on the membership automatically — no manual syncing.

If a membership has no benefits or no active plans, Sailia displays a reminder so you know what's missing before launch.

<Note>
  Session discount benefits run before any other [discount codes](/pricing/discount-codes#stacking-behavior) in the stack, so a manual code applies on top of the already-reduced member price.
</Note>

## In short

- Benefits define what members get.
- Three types: **Session discount**, **General**, and **Ticket access**.
- They apply automatically and can be freely combined.
