Sailia's communication editor supports reusable building blocks called **saved blocks**, plus a broader template system you can use for far more than just booking confirmations.

## Saved blocks

Saved blocks are sections of email content you can reuse across templates — perfect for headers, footers, signatures, "what to bring" lists, and any other consistent content. They keep your branding consistent and save time when building new templates.

### Save a block

When you build an email template, select a section in the editor and click **Save as block**. The block is now available to insert into any other template.

### Use a saved block

Inside any template, drag in a saved block from the **Blocks** library. Updating the saved block updates it everywhere it's used.

<Tip>
  Build saved blocks for: branded header, branded footer, social media links, *What to bring*, *How to find us*, *Cancellation policy summary*. Once these exist, every new template takes minutes to assemble.
</Tip>

## Beyond customer confirmations

Communication templates aren't just for booking confirmations. Common other uses:

### Bookings

- Personalised basket-abandonment reminders
- Group booking organiser prompts

### Waivers

- Personalised [waiver](/customers/waivers) reminders for incomplete documents

### Staff

- Inform staff which sessions they're delivering
- Onboarding sequences for new staff
- Availability requests
- Upcoming shift reminders
- Timesheet and invoicing prompts

For the events that can trigger these messages, see [workflow triggers](/integrations/workflow-triggers).
