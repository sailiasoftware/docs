Qualifications let you define the certifications and competencies required for your activities. You can create qualification types and assign them to staff members to ensure the right people are leading the right activities.

Sailia supports two qualification categories:

| Category | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Instructor** | Certifications required to lead or instruct activities (e.g. surf instructor level 2, first aid) |
| **Personal competency** | Skills or competencies that staff need for specific roles or tasks |

## Create a qualification

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open settings">
    Navigate to **Settings** and select the **Qualifications** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a qualification">
    Click **Add Qualification** and enter the qualification name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the type">
    Select whether this is an **Instructor** qualification or a **Personal Competency**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save the qualification. You can now assign it to [staff members](/staff/management).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manage qualifications

From the qualifications settings, you can:

- View all defined qualifications
- Edit a qualification's name or type
- Delete qualifications that are no longer needed

<Warning>
  Deleting a qualification removes it from all staff members who have it assigned. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

## Assign qualifications to staff

Once qualifications are defined, assign them to individual staff members from their [staff profile](/staff/management). This helps you track which staff members are certified for which activities and ensures compliance with any regulatory requirements.
